- Horn of Blasting — 50% greater damage
- Lyre of Building — Double effects
- Pipes of the Sewer — Double number of rats in one-half the usual time

Bards will never serve another as a henchman for longer than 1 to 4 months. They are unable to employ henchmen other than druids, fighters

or thieves of human, half-eleven, or elven race. It is possible for a bard to have 1 henchman upon attaining 5th level, 2 henchmen can be maintained upon reaching 8th level, 3 at 11th, 4 at 14th, 5 at 17th, 6 at 20th and any number at 23rd. This is subject to the bard’s charisma rating, of course. Only bards of 23rd level will settle down and construct a stronghold of any sort.

**Note:** If bards are permitted in your campaign, there is a possibility that your DM will also include certain magical items usable only by bards.
